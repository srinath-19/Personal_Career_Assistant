Pursuing a Master of Science in Computer Science at the University
of Colorado Boulder, I am an AI Engineer at Inovonics, where
I contribute to developing an innovative full-stack AI system for
converting 2D floor plans into structured digital building maps. My
work focuses on using YOLOv12-OBB for structural detection,
integrating a robust backend, task queues, databases, and front-
end frameworks to support applications in safety, automation, and
sensing for senior living and commercial spaces.
My professional interests lie in applying large language models
(LLMs), MLOps, and retrieval-augmented generation (RAG)
technologies. I value creating efficient, reliable, and scalable
systems, driving impactful solutions through collaboration and
iteration. Currently, I am focused on advancing technologies at the
intersection of AI and MLOps, which enable production-ready LLM
applications through robust data pipelines, evaluation, deployment,
and monitoring. I am especially interested in building end-to-end
systems that improve retrieval quality, reduce latency and cost, and
deliver measurable impact in real-world products.
